Seattle Attractions:

  1. Space Needle

    2. This is the icon of the city! An amazing observation tower in Seattle. Here you can take picturess of the city and enjoy the great bar and glass floor experience!

  2. Museum of Pop Culture (MoPOP)

    3. This is a place to go if you love pop culture! Great exhibitions of the greatest bands from Seattle area may be found here. Thiss building architecture is also stunning! Here, you can take lots of pictures! And take advantage of the various exhibitions that they have each year!

  3. Chihuly Garden and Glass

    4. This place is completely amazing! Here, you can see the studio glass of Dale Chihuly. If you enjoy art, this is agreat place to go!

  4. Freemont Troll

    5. The Freemont Troll is a public sculpture located under a bridge. This troll has appeared in films such as "10 Things I Hate About You," which was filmed in Seattle.

  5. Kerry Park

    6. Here you can see the skyline of Seattle! This is a small park located in Queen Ann neighborhood! If you are lucky, you could see Mt Rainier!
